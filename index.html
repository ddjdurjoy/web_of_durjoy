About me

I’m Durjoy Das Joy, born on the serene evening of July 13, in the heart of Chatak, Sunamganj, Bangladesh. Despite my deep roots in Chatak, life’s journey, influenced by my father’s dedication to his role as a sub-inspector in the Bangladesh Police, led us to Moulvibazar. It was here, amidst the nurturing environments of school, college, and the warmth of family life with my two sisters, that my story truly began to unfold.

From a young age, I found myself drawn not to the paths well-trodden but to the realms of programming, technology, and late-night literary explorations. While I initially expressed myself through the art of writing, my curiosity and passion for technology soon steered me towards a new horizon: web development.

The year 2015 marked a significant turn in my journey. Amidst the personal challenge of losing my father, I found solace and a sense of direction in the logical and creative world of coding. Web development transcended being a mere interest; it became my calling, a canvas for my creativity and an avenue for innovation.

By May 27, 2023, I took a definitive step further into my passion by immersing myself in the global web development community. This wasn’t just about honing my skills; it was about connecting, learning, and growing with fellow enthusiasts and professionals who share my zeal for making the digital world more accessible, engaging, and beautiful.

Today, as I navigate through the rigors of academia and the competitive job market, I find unwavering joy and purpose in the intricacies of web development. Each project, each line of code, represents a piece of me, embodying my strategic approach, my problem-solving capabilities, and my relentless pursuit of excellence.

In this journey of web development, as in life, I continue to forge ahead, fueled by my passion for technology and an unyielding desire to leave a mark on the digital landscape.
